Leveraging the expertise we have acquired over many years in the global logistics business, Azuma is able to provide safe, secure and speedy integrated transportation services (between 3 countries) for export using optimal routes based on tie-ups between our overseas bases and on-site partners. These services range from the freight of plant construction and ODA-related materials and equipment to packing and customs clearance, as well as maritime, railroad and truck transportation and even the export of cargo to the actual site. In particular, we have considerable experience in transportation in areas where infrastructure is not yet fully developed. Please feel free to inquire about the transportation of any cargo you may require, including heavy, large or hazardous items.

We have continually provided transportation services for various large construction machineries including those used in building, paving, laying and mining. Bonded transportation across borders involves not only a number of logistical difficulties, but also incurs an unimaginable amount of costs and procedures. With our accumulated experience in transportation services in such challenging environments and knowledge of the particular features of each type of machineries, you can rest assured that we will safely deliver your cargo to the required destination.

Ship chartering is necessary for the integrated transportation of project cargo and heavy and large objects that cannot fit into containers. It also requires establishing and maintaining a sophisticated and excellent network in order to allocate the appropriate type of cargo ships owned by reliable owners at the right time of planned shipment on the conditions, including freight, advantageous to customers. We can also readily receive the requests for a part of integrated international transportation services or maritime transportation services only.

Through joint investment we have established "Trans Russia Agency" Japan as the Japanese agent of Russia's largest liner shipping company, FESCO (Far-Eastern Shipping Company). We are now able to offer integrated transportation services of FESCO charters landing in Russia, along with integrated transportation services based on our own network and using Russian and Chinese railroads to Mongolia and former Soviet Union countries (Central Asian countries) including Russia.

What sets apart our LCL cargo services is that unlike the network of other companies that cover most major cities around the globe, Azuma offers LCL services specializing in the Mediterranean Sea and Yantai region. Please be sure to inquire about our services if you have a less-than container load (LCL) of cargo to deliver, and you need information on the destination, Yantai (China) or Mediterranean Sea ports, by reason of unfamiliarity with them.
